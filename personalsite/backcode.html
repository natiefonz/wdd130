/ why create this site?

Being a computer major has me creating many projects. Making a personal website that benefits my future career would be useful. I can showcase my work and progress, even the website itself can be something on its own to showcase. As I continue with my education and learn new skills, I can edit my website as I go to keep it up to date. For now, my page will be a jumble of many small projects, but as I get deeper into my major, I will have bigger projects that will be able to showcase my true capabilities. How else would I be able to show my future employer everything I can do in one place? The simple answer is creating a website that holds all the information they need to know about me.

/ how often do I update the website?

I am updating this website every couple of weeks. Every couple of weeks I create something new and then with the new project I upload it to the website. I am not going to post everything I have created, just the projects that show improvement and progress in my learning. If there is something specific you would like to see, just submit the suggestion on the left.

/ who is the intended audience?

Trying to locate everything I have created is going to be hard. Especially, if I have some projects on Git Hub, saved locally, uploaded on social media (i.e., LinkedIn). Having one spot for all projects will make it easy for me to showcase everything I am capable of. I plan on choosing some of the projects I have created and publishing them on my website for easy reference.
