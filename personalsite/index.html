About the Creator

Hello! My name is Natelie Fonnesbeck. I am currently a Junior at Brigham Young University - Idaho studying Communication with a minor in Computer Information Technology. I love finding ways to innovate and improve things I interact with. Whether it is through code or communication, I like testing the boundaries of efficiency.

I enjoy any project that sparks my creativity. I am actively learning web design and computer languages such as Python, C#, and JavaScript. This website is a way for me to showcase some of the code I have created both for career and teaching purposes.

Experience

I am still new and learning but I am hoping this website will inspire people just starting their coding journey to look beyond what they think they are capable by showing them examples of what is possible.
