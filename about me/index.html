About Me

Natelie Fonnesbeck

Hello everyone! My name is Natelie. I currently live in Rexburg, ID with my husband and three cats. I have three younger brothers who are all in different stages of life. I also have a four step-sisters and one step brother! They are all also younger than me.

I am pursuing a degree in communication with a minor in CIT. I have really been enjoying it. I also work full time as an assistant team lead at a utility billing company. The combination of these two things makes my life a lovely mix of hectic and busy. If I am being honest I would not prefer it any other way.

In the rare occasion that I have free time, I like to play video games, watch a bit of television, play volleyball, listen to music, or bother my cats. I also love to read. My guilty pleasure is fantasy romance but I also love horror.

I am excited to learn with all of you! Feel free to ask me any questions! I love book, music, and movie recommendations. I dabble in a little bit of everything so it is hard to find something I will not enjoy.
